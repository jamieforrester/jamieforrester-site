what these examples show
// What these examples show structural extraction

The same structural pattern appears across both systems: a critical function is absent at the point of consequence. In both cases, correction loops operated inside the broken frame rather than on it. In both cases, hidden human carriers absorbed the load the architecture could not carry. In both cases, the primitive had never been corrected — only the interfaces around it.

Two domains. Two full pipelines. Diagnosis followed by redesign in each case. The method is the same. The structural pattern beneath the failure is the same. What changes is the system under analysis.
