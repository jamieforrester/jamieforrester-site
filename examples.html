Examples

Public proof sets for structural diagnosis and redesign.

Each case presents a complete four-artifact deliverable set. Three artifacts form the public proof layer (Translation, Diagnostic, and Redesign Executive Summary). The fourth — the full Redesign Artifact — is the restricted builder specification available only through a Full Structural Engagement.

These are professional deliverables, not marketing case studies or slide decks. Produced independently from public evidence. Not commissioned by the organisations analysed.

The point is not volume. It is repeatability. The same method produces governed artifacts for both a private AI platform and a national healthcare system.

Each Diagnostic Artifact follows the same structural movement: system boundary → workflow trace → functional diagnosis → governing diagnosis → correction dependency order. Each Redesign Artifact then converts that diagnosis into corrected primitive, replacement architecture, refusal invariants, transition sequence, recapture gates, and proof conditions.

Proof of structural capability, not contracted outcomes.

Outlier AI

AI task marketplace · private platform
Governing Diagnosis

Outlier recruits through the grammar of an expertise market while operating through the mechanics of a reliability pipeline. In plain terms: the platform recruits experts to do compliance work. That is why surface corrections have not resolved the failure.

Replacement direction

Replace the single contributor frame with a dual-stream contributor coordination system: Stream A for constrained reliability execution, Stream B for bounded expert judgment, connected by a portable contributor continuity record.

Produced independently from public evidence. Not commissioned by Outlier or Scale AI.

NHS Primary Care Continuity System

Public healthcare institution · National Health Service (UK)
Governing Diagnosis

NHS primary care is record-rich and continuity-poor. Operational memory is not structurally present at the point of clinical consequence, so the patient becomes the record.

Replacement direction

Replace fragmented institutional memory with a patient-held operational continuity system: portable continuity record, symptom event layer, Consultation Packet, outcome append, emergency access, carer access, and recognition pathway.

Produced independently from public evidence. Not commissioned by the NHS, NHS England, or DHSC.

what these examples show

The domain changes. The structural method does not.

In both systems, a critical function is absent at the point where consequence must be carried. In Outlier, contributor continuity and work-class truth are absent at the point of routing, scoring, and consequence. In NHS primary care, operational memory is absent at the point of clinical decision.

Hidden human carriers absorb what the architecture cannot hold. Queue Managers, contributors, and external tooling carry platform coherence in Outlier. Patients, carers, known GPs, and informal practice staff carry continuity in NHS primary care.

In both cases, correction remained inside the inherited frame. More support, more compliance, more digital surface, more process. The primitive remained untouched.

The artifacts demonstrate the same conversion across domains: recurring failure → structural diagnosis → replacement architecture → proof condition.

This is the redesign law: diagnosis can name the structural failure, but correction loops operating inside the existing frame cannot generate the primitive change the system requires.

Why the full Redesign Artifact is restricted

The public proof layer is sufficient to demonstrate the method, evidence boundary, governing diagnosis, and replacement logic. The full Redesign Artifact is restricted because it contains the build-value layer: transition sequence, refusal invariants, admissibility conditions, recapture gates, pilot validity rules, object-level specifications, and builder handoff.

Public artifacts demonstrate capability. Restricted specifications define the full replacement.

Restricted Redesign Artifacts are not published as sample documents. Access can be requested, but the full specification is not a general resource.
