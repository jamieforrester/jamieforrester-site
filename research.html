01 // Theoretical foundations the two core models

Two models sit beneath the entire body of work. The Four-Function Law explains how institutions fail under scale. The Universal Formula explains how a primitive becomes a civilisation-layer. Both are used in every engagement to locate where the failure actually belongs before the artifact is written.

Four-Function Law

Why institutions fail when functions fuse

Sensing, Interpretation, Authority, and Memory must remain structurally separate for an institution to scale. When they fuse into single human nodes — as they almost always do — the system becomes brittle, self-referential, and unable to respond correctly to external pressure.

This framework sits directly behind how consequence, interpretation, authority, and continuity are located inside the Diagnostic Artifact. Locating which function has fused, and where, is typically what resolves the diagnostic question.

Universal Formula

How a primitive becomes a civilisation layer

Purpose → Primitive → Medium → OS → Marketplace → Category → Civilization → Meta

A diagnostic and construction sequence that tracks how any durable system scales. Each layer has distinct structural requirements. Failure is almost always a mismatch between the layer a system is operating at and the layer it thinks it is at.

Used in both engagements to identify which layer the failure belongs to before the primitive, boundary, or redesign sequence is defined. A system cannot be correctly redesigned until its actual layer is identified.

03 // Seeing Systems book — fully drafted

Seeing Systems

Drafted

Why a 10-year-old can understand what organisations can't.

A carrier-based method for systemic literacy across 5 parts and 30 chapters. The book makes structural thinking accessible without requiring a technical background — using familiar objects to reveal the actions hiding inside complex systems. Once you can name the primitive, the system stops feeling mysterious.

The 60-second reading tool Four questions. One sentence each.
01 Purpose What does this system actually exist to do? One sentence. Ignore what it says about itself.
02 Action What is the one thing it performs over and over? That repeated action is the primitive.
03 Rules What protects or constrains that action? If you can't see the rules, watch what stops things from moving.
04 Failure What breaks first? Who notices? Who pays the cost? Systems reveal themselves at their edges.

04 // The System Founder Framework book — in design

The System Founder Framework

In design

A construction doctrine for OS-level building in the AI era.

Formula, ladder, toolchain, and playbooks for building durable systems without drifting into theatre. Applies the Universal Formula as a construction sequence rather than a diagnostic tool. Where Seeing Systems teaches structural literacy, this book provides the build methodology.

05 // Applied builds systems in development

The practice is not purely advisory. These are live systems built on the same structural logic — law-first design, explicit rules, constrained ownership, deterministic pipelines. They are included here because building real systems under real constraints is part of how the diagnostic lens stays accurate. You cannot correctly diagnose what you have never had to build.

Spectrum Registry

In development

A law-first symbolic registry built for durable truth and constrained ownership flows.

Designed from the primitive outward: what the minimal unit of truth is, how ownership is constrained by rule rather than convention, and how the registry resists drift as it scales. The structural decisions made during this build directly inform how boundaries and enforcement are analysed in the Diagnostic Artifact.

PromptFactory

In development

A deterministic build pipeline that pushes intelligence upstream into specification and verification.

Built on the principle that ambiguity should be resolved at specification time, not execution time. The same preference for legibility, explicit rules, and failure-resistant design that appears in the written artifacts appears here as engineering decisions.
